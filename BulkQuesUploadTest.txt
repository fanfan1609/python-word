	1. 	Jim Jordan is an appraiser. As such, he
	A.	discovers value
	B.	insures value.
	C.	estimates value.

ANSWER:C

	A.	Are worth exactly the same amount.
	B.	can be combined.
	C.	have the same highest and best use.
	D.	are identical.

ANSWER:D

	3.	The appraiser who works for a number of different clients is known as a(n)
	A.	fee appraiser.
	B.	freelance appraiser.
	C.	staff appraiser.
	D.	in-house appraiser.

ANSWER:A
